Types of Tags in HTML

Go to bottom

Container Tags:

Tags which don't have their own content.
eg.- table, body, head, div

Standalone Tags:

Tags which need not to be closed.
eg.- br, img, hr

Content Tags:

Tags that must be opened and closed and the content is between opening and closing tag.
eg.- h1, td, p, b, i

Parent & Child Tags:

Tags that follow hierarchy.
eg.- table, tr, td

Attribute Tags:

Tags that have attributes.
eg.- id, class, style
